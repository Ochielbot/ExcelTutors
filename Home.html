What we're doing

Bridging the education gap.

We leverage our skills in programming and forbearance in educating to provide those who are typically forgotten with access to good quality education. Whether this is due to physical and mental disabilities or simply a lack of an understanding of the larger educational system's inner workings, we will be there.

The quality of education given to students should not depend on their unique abilities or their financial status and neither should the benefits gained from said education. This is the principal thought process behind Excel Tutors and we hope to bring about a reality where nobody is forgotten as both quality of education and technology continue their rate of rapid improvement.

We don't fit in​ one box.

We believe that learning is a life-long journey and we do our best to help at the key stages that we, as a global network of high-achieving students, are most experienced in.

Coaching

We offer student specific tutoring for a select-few individuals while also offering generalized educational materials.

Counselling

We guide prospective international college students throughout every stage of their admissions process.

Careers

We link students to professionals in their field and guide them towards the skills they need for their desired profession.

The "M-word"

Money.

The single greatest barrier to providing good quality education to those from less developed communities is money and we make that very clear to the students we work with. From that point on we pursue every avenue possible to get the student over this barrier.

our dream

What's the endgame?

Educate

The first step which we are currenlty at is getting quality education to those who would otherwise be unable to access it.

Inspire

We are living examples that the educated seek to help educate and thus the second step is to create an obsessive culture of educating the less-fortunate which would in turn breed innovation of all forms.

transform

The final stage would be the knock-on effect of the previous stage. Young minds from lesser-off backgrounds are educated and with the skills gained from their education they would innovate, this process would continue ideally to the point where there would be nobody left unable to access good quality education.
